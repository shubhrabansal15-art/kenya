Total CO₂ Emissions - Kenya

Kenya's total CO₂ output has grown steadily, driven primarily by population growth, expanding transport infrastructure and increased energy demand. Despite this rise, absolute volumes remain low by global standards, Kenya contributes under 0.1% of global emissions.

Per Capita Emissions
CO₂ per Capita - Kenya
tonnes CO₂ per person · annual · Our World in Data

Per-capita emissions reveal a more nuanced picture than totals alone. Kenya's figure has remained remarkably stable — well below the global average of ~4.7 t/person, reflecting the clean electricity mix (dominated by geothermal and hydro) and a largely rural, low-consumption economy.
